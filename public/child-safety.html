NebulaNet Child Safety Standards

NebulaNet is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitment

NebulaNet strictly prohibits any content, behavior, or activity that involves child sexual abuse material (CSAM), sexual exploitation of minors, or grooming behaviors. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward CSAE.

Safety Measures

NebulaNet provides user reporting tools, account blocking, moderation actions, and enforcement measures including content removal and account suspension.

Reporting CSAE

Users may report child safety concerns directly within the NebulaNet app. Confirmed CSAM is reported to appropriate regional and national authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC), where required.

Contact

For CSAE or child safety concerns, contact us at: support@nebulanet.space
