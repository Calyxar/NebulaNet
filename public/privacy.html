NebulaNet Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 7, 2026

Overview

NebulaNet is a social media app that lets users create accounts, share posts and stories, and interact with others. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email, username, and profile details you provide.
  • User content: posts, stories, comments, messages, and uploaded media.
  • App activity: likes, follows, saves, shares, views, and related engagement.
  • Diagnostics: crash/performance logs to improve reliability.

How we use information

  • Provide app functionality and customer support.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Maintain safety, prevent abuse, and enforce rules.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers needed to operate the app.

Account deletion

You can request account deletion by emailing: support@nebulanet.space

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: support@nebulanet.space
