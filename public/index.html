BUILT
DIFFERENT

Every feature was chosen with one goal — making real connection feel effortless. No algorithm tricks. No dark patterns. Just the people and content you actually care about.

01
Posts & stories

Photos, videos, GIFs, and polls. Stories vanish after 24 hours — gradient rings show you who posted before you've even tapped.

02
Real-time chat

Direct messages with media sharing, read receipts, and reactions. Start a conversation from any profile in seconds.

03
Communities

Interest-based groups with their own feeds. Find your people — whether that's dev, art, gaming, or anything in between.

04
Events

Create public or private events, set capacity, invite your community. In-person or online — all in one place.

05
Privacy controls

Private accounts, follow requests, block, mute, and granular settings for who sees what. Your data, your rules.

06
Trending topics

See what's moving in your network right now. Tap any hashtag or @mention to dive into the conversation happening around it.

Use the in-app feedback or reply in the Google Group. Every report gets read.
